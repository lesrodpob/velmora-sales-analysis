# Velmora – Sales & Customer Analysis

## Objective
Analyze sales performance and customer behavior to identify growth opportunities.

## Tools
- Python (pandas, matplotlib)
- Kaggle

## Key Insights
- Sales show a positive growth trend
- Home category drives most revenue
- A small group of customers generates a large portion of sales

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on top-performing products
- Improve customer retention strategies
- Increase average order value
